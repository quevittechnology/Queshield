# Privacy Policy for QueShield

**Last Updated:** December 14, 2024

## Introduction

QueShield ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile security application.

## Our Privacy Commitment

**We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

QueShield is designed with privacy as the top priority:
- ✅ All security scanning happens locally on your device
- ✅ No data is sent to external servers
- ✅ No user accounts or registration required
- ✅ No analytics or tracking
- ✅ No advertisements

## Data Handled Locally

QueShield accesses the following data **only on your device**:

### File System Access
- **Purpose:** Scan files for malware and threats
- **Storage:** Threat records stored locally in encrypted database
- **Sharing:** Never shared with anyone

### Phone & SMS Access
- **Purpose:** Detect spam calls and fraudulent messages
- **Storage:** Blocked numbers stored locally on device
- **Sharing:** Never shared with anyone

### Location Access
- **Purpose:** Lost phone tracking feature (only when enabled by user)
- **Storage:** Last known location stored locally
- **Sharing:** Only sent to trusted contact number you configure (via SMS)

### App Information
- **Purpose:** Detect fake payment apps and security threats
- **Storage:** App analysis results stored locally
- **Sharing:** Never shared with anyone

## Permissions Required

QueShield requests the following permissions:

- **Storage:** To scan files for malware
- **Location:** For lost phone tracking (optional)
- **Phone/SMS:** For spam call and message detection
- **Notifications:** To alert you about threats
- **Network:** For web security URL checks (local only)

**All permissions are used exclusively for security features and never for data collection.**

## Third-Party Services

QueShield does NOT use any third-party services, including:
- ❌ No analytics (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
- ❌ No ad networks
- ❌ No cloud services
- ❌ No social media integrations

## Data Retention

All data is stored locally on your device and is deleted when you:
- Uninstall the app
- Clear app data
- Factory reset your device

## Children's Privacy

QueShield does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.
